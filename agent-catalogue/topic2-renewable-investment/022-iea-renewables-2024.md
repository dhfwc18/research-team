# IEA -- Renewables 2024: Analysis and Forecast to 2030

**Organisation:** International Energy Agency (IEA)
**Date:** October 9, 2024

## Description

IEA annual medium-term renewable energy market report covering electricity, heat, and transport
sectors with forecasts to 2030. Projects massive global renewable capacity growth sufficient to
match entire power capacity of major economies, advancing toward the COP28 tripling target.
New chapter on renewable fuels (bioenergy, hydrogen, e-fuels). Includes manufacturing trends,
technology costs, grid integration, and company prospects. Companion Renewables 2024 Dataset
available (Excel; premium electricity sector data). CC BY 4.0.

## Key Metrics

- Forecast period: to 2030
- Tracks progress toward COP28 renewable tripling target
- Dataset includes technology cost trajectories

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Provides technology cost trajectories and capacity growth forecasts to 2030,
directly relevant to the investment model. Cost data informs the renewable competitiveness
side of the oil-renewable substitution analysis.

## Possible Application

- Renewable technology cost curves for model calibration
- Capacity growth forecasts for scenario construction
- Investment cost vs Brent price competitiveness analysis
