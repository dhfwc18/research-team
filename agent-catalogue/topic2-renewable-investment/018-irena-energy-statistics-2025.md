# IRENA -- Renewable Energy Statistics 2025

**Organisation:** International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA)
**Date:** July 2025

## Description

IRENA's comprehensive annual statistics report covering renewable energy capacity, generation,
and in some editions investment. Complements the capacity statistics publication with generation
data by technology and country. The page returned 403 on fetch. Data is available through the
IRENASTAT platform with country-level Excel downloads.

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Generation data complements capacity statistics. Together with capacity data,
allows computation of capacity utilisation trends which reflect relative competitiveness
of renewables vs fossil fuels.

## Possible Application

- Renewable generation time series by technology and country
- Capacity utilisation trends for cost competitiveness analysis
- Cross-validation of investment flow data against deployment outcomes
