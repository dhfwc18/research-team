# IEA -- World Energy Investment 2024

**Organisation:** International Energy Agency (IEA)
**Date:** June 6, 2024

## Description

IEA flagship annual investment report tracking capital flows across the global energy sector.
The 2024 edition covers 2023 actuals and 2024 initial estimates. Key finding: clean energy
investment in 2024 is set to be twice the amount going to fossil fuels. Coverage includes
fuel and electricity supply, critical minerals, energy efficiency, R&D, and energy financing
across 10 major economies and regions. Full PDF and supplementary datafile freely available
under CC BY 4.0.

## Key Metrics

- Clean energy investment approximately 2x fossil fuel investment in 2024
- Includes supplementary methodology annex and associated datafile

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Essential source for the clean/renewable vs fossil fuel investment comparison.
The associated datafile provides structured investment data for econometric use.

## Possible Application

- Clean energy investment time series vs oil price movements
- Clean/fossil investment ratio as dependent variable for regression
- Regional investment breakdown for panel data analysis
