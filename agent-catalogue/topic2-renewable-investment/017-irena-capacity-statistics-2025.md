# IRENA -- Renewable Capacity Statistics 2025

**Organisation:** International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA)
**Date:** March 2025

## Description

IRENA's annual renewable capacity statistics publication, covering installed capacity
by technology (solar PV, wind, hydro, bioenergy, geothermal, marine) and by country/region.
Data typically spans from 2000 to the most recent year (2024 in this edition). The page
returned 403 on fetch. IRENA makes this data freely available as an Excel download through
their IRENASTAT database platform (irena.org/IRENASTAT).

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Installed capacity data proxies actual renewable investment deployment.
Useful for validating investment flows against physical buildout and for computing
capacity factors and unit costs.

## Possible Application

- Renewable capacity growth series to compare against Brent price movements
- Technology-level deployment data for disaggregated regression analysis
- Capacity vs investment cost decomposition
