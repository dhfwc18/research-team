# IEA -- World Energy Investment 2025

**Organisation:** International Energy Agency (IEA)
**Date:** June 5, 2025

## Description

The 10th edition of the IEA World Energy Investment report, covering 2024 investment actuals
and initial 2025 projections. Expanded regional coverage (US, Latin America, EU, Africa,
Middle East, Eurasia, China, India, Japan, Korea, Southeast Asia). Includes an Investment
Data Explorer tool for interactive analysis and a downloadable World Energy Investment 2025
Datafile. Available under CC BY 4.0.

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Most recent IEA investment data available (2024 actuals). The Investment
Data Explorer and downloadable datafile are particularly useful for the Data Analyst.

## Possible Application

- Most recent clean/renewable investment benchmark (2024)
- Investment Data Explorer for interactive scenario comparison
- Regional investment breakdown for panel analysis

## Download Note

PDF and World Energy Investment 2025 Datafile freely available under CC BY 4.0. The datafile
is the key structured data resource.
