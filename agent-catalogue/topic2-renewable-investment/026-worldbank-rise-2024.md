# World Bank -- Regulatory Indicators for Sustainable Energy (RISE) 2024

**Organisation:** World Bank / Energy Sector Management Assistance Program (ESMAP)
**Date:** 2025 (data as of 2024)

## Description

RISE is the World Bank's regulatory assessment framework measuring government policy support
for sustainable energy across three dimensions: energy access, energy efficiency, and renewable
energy. Covers 140+ countries with scoring on policy pillars (renewable energy targets,
grid access, net metering, carbon pricing, etc.). The page loaded with limited content on
fetch. The RISE database is freely accessible at esmap.org/rise with country scorecards
and downloadable Excel data.

## Usefulness Assessment

**Partially relevant** -- RISE indicators can serve as policy environment controls in the
renewable investment regression. Countries with higher RISE scores attract more investment
independent of oil price levels.

## Possible Application

- Policy environment control variable for panel regression
- Regulatory quality index for investment climate assessment
- Cross-country heterogeneity in oil-renewable investment relationship

## Access Note

Full dataset available at: https://rise.esmap.org (Excel download by country and indicator)
