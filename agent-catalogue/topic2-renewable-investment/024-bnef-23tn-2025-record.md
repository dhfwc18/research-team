# BloombergNEF -- Global Energy Transition Investment Reached Record $2.3 Trillion in 2025

**Organisation:** BloombergNEF
**Date:** January 26, 2026

## Description

BNEF press release announcing that global energy transition investment reached a record
$2.3 trillion in 2025, up 8% from 2024. This is the broadest headline energy transition
figure (covers solar, wind, EVs, storage, electrified heat, nuclear, carbon capture, hydrogen,
sustainable shipping and aviation, power grids). Full sector and regional breakdown available
in the full BNEF report (subscription required). The press release provides the headline
figure and year-on-year change.

## Key Metrics

- Total energy transition investment 2025: USD 2.3 trillion
- Year-on-year increase: 8% from 2024

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Most recent (2025) headline energy transition investment figure. Provides
the latest data point for the investment time series and contextualises IRENA's renewable-
specific figure.

## Possible Application

- Most recent energy transition investment headline (2025 full-year)
- Benchmark for time series endpoint
- Cross-check against IRENA (renewable-only) and IEA (energy-sector) figures
