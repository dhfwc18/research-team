# ECB -- Economy-Wide Climate Stress Test (OP 281)

**Organisation:** European Central Bank
**Date:** September 2021

## Description

ECB's pioneering economy-wide climate stress test paper. PDF is 1.4 MB; binary content
on fetch. Covers both transition and physical risk assessment for the eurozone economy
and financial system. Employs a granular firm-level approach combined with macro-financial
modelling. Key contribution: linking climate scenarios to sectoral firm profitability
and then to bank credit losses. A widely cited methodological reference for climate stress
testing globally.

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Methodological reference for economy-wide climate stress testing. The
granular firm-to-system transmission approach is directly applicable to the project's
stress-test design for renewable energy investment under different Brent price scenarios.

## Possible Application

- Economy-wide stress-testing methodology (firm-level + macro linkage)
- Scenario design reference for orderly vs disorderly transition
- Physical + transition risk decomposition for comprehensive scenario coverage
