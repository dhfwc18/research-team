# IMF -- Climate-Related Stress Testing: Transition Risk in Colombia (WP/21/261)

**Organisation:** International Monetary Fund
**Date:** November 2021

## Description

IMF Working Paper applying climate transition risk stress testing to Colombia, an oil-producing
developing country with significant renewable energy potential. Page returned 403. Based on
the WP/20/232 Norway companion paper, this applies a similar IMF framework to a developing-
country context. Colombia's fiscal dependence on oil revenues and rapidly growing solar/wind
sector make it a useful comparison case for the project.

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Developing-country oil-producer case study for transition risk. Provides a
methodological template applicable to developing economy stress-testing scenarios.

## Possible Application

- Developing economy transition risk stress-testing framework
- Fiscal impact of oil price decline on renewable investment capacity
- Comparison case for emerging market heterogeneity in the analysis
