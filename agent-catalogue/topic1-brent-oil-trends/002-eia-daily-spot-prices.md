# EIA Daily Spot Prices for Crude Oil and Petroleum Products

**Organisation:** U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)

## Description

Daily, weekly, monthly, and annual spot price data for crude oil (WTI and Brent) and petroleum
products (RBOB gasoline, heating oil, diesel, jet fuel, propane) at various U.S. delivery
points. Date range: 1986-2026 for most series; RBOB from 2003, diesel from 2006.
Units: crude oil in USD/barrel, products in USD/gallon. Excel historical series download
available. Last updated 2026-04-01.

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Daily Brent price data enables higher-frequency analysis and volatility
modelling. Complements the monthly EIA series with intra-year resolution.

## Possible Application

- High-frequency Brent price series for GARCH volatility modelling
- Daily price shock identification aligned with renewable investment event studies
- Cross-check for the monthly Brent historical series

## Download Note

Excel download readily available from the page. File size is negligible (well under 50MB).
