# EIA -- Brent Crude Oil Prices Averaged $19 Per Barrel Less in 2023 Than 2022

**Organisation:** U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)
**Date:** January 2024

## Description

EIA Today in Energy article analysing the Brent crude oil price decline from 2022 to 2023.
Brent averaged $83/barrel in 2023, down from $101/barrel in 2022 (a $19/barrel decline).
Key drivers identified: global market adjustment to Russian crude oil trade rerouting post-EU
ban; global demand shortfall relative to expectations; OPEC+ extended production cuts through
2024; Saudi voluntary cuts of 1 million bbl/day; late-year Red Sea shipping attack impact.
Article includes a Brent daily spot price chart (data sourced from Bloomberg). No downloadable
dataset; chart only.
