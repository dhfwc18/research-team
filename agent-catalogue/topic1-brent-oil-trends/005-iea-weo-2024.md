# IEA World Energy Outlook 2024

**Organisation:** International Energy Agency (IEA)
**Date:** October 16, 2024

## Description

The IEA's flagship annual report on global energy trends and projections. The 2024 edition
examines energy security amid Middle East tensions and geopolitical uncertainty, clean energy
transition progress, and climate objectives. Covers three main scenarios plus sensitivity
analyses for renewables, electric mobility, LNG, heatwaves, efficiency, and AI electricity
demand impact. Highlights rising investment in clean technologies and rapid electricity demand
growth. Full PDF download available. AI-powered exploration tool included on site.

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Essential long-term policy and investment context. Provides scenario frameworks
(Stated Policies, Announced Pledges, Net Zero) that directly inform macroeconomic scenario
construction for the Brent-renewables analysis.

## Possible Application

- Long-term energy transition scenarios for model stress-testing
- Renewable energy investment volume benchmarks by scenario
- Complementary oil price pathway assumptions per IEA scenario
