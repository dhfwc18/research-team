# Our World in Data -- Crude Oil Spot Prices (Inflation-Adjusted, 1861-2025)

**Organisation:** Our World in Data / Energy Institute / S&P Global Platts
**Date:** Updated June 27, 2025

## Description

Interactive dataset of crude oil spot prices from 1861 to 2024 (latest data year), sourced
from the Energy Institute's Statistical Review of World Energy (2025 edition), based on S&P
Global Platts data. Units are USD per cubic meter. Offers CSV download (full dataset or
filtered), JSON metadata API, and code examples for Python, R, Excel, and Stata.

This is an exceptionally long historical series (165 years) enabling structural analysis
across multiple commodity cycles, wars, and energy transitions.

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- The longest available continuous crude oil price series, ideal for long-run
structural analysis and calibrating historical price regimes.

## Possible Application

- Long-run historical price context for model calibration
- Identifying historical analogues for current price regimes
- Inflation-adjusted price series for real-terms modelling
