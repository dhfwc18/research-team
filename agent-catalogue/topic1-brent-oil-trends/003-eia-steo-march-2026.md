# EIA Short-Term Energy Outlook (STEO) -- March 2026

**Organisation:** U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)
**Date:** March 2026

## Description

The EIA STEO is a monthly publication providing 18-month forecasts for U.S. and global energy
markets. The March 2026 edition covers Brent crude oil price projections, global liquid fuels
supply and demand balances, natural gas, electricity, coal, and renewables. The PDF is 6.8 MB
and could not be text-extracted on fetch (binary PDF); content is known from EIA documentation.
Typically includes tables of Brent price forecasts (monthly, quarterly) and macroeconomic
assumptions.
