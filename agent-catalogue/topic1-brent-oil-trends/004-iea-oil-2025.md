# IEA Oil 2025: Analysis and Forecast to 2030

**Organisation:** International Energy Agency (IEA)
**Date:** 2025

## Description

IEA's medium-term oil market report covering global oil supply, demand, refining, and trade
forecasts to 2030. The PDF is 4.7 MB; binary content could not be text-extracted on fetch.
Based on IEA's published scope, the report includes oil demand scenarios across sectors
(transport, industry, petrochemicals), supply projections by region, and analysis of the
clean energy transition's impact on long-run oil demand. Typically includes Brent oil price
assumptions underlying the forecast.
