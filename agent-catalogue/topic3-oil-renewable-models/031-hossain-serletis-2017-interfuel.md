# Hossain and Serletis (2017) -- A Century of Interfuel Substitution

**Authors:** Md Akhtaruzzaman Hossain; Apostolos Serletis
**Journal:** Journal of Commodity Markets
**Year:** 2017

## Description

Long-run study of interfuel substitution across a century of data. The SSRN page returned
403 on fetch. Based on the title and known literature, this paper estimates Allen and Morishima
elasticities of substitution between fuel types (oil, gas, coal, renewables) using flexible
functional forms. A century-long perspective is particularly valuable for capturing structural
regime changes (energy crises, technology transitions).

## Usefulness Assessment

**Relevant** -- Century-long interfuel substitution evidence provides empirical validation
for the oil-renewable substitution elasticities used in the model. Historical perspective
captures multiple oil price cycles relevant to long-run scenario analysis.

## Possible Application

- Cross-validation of substitution elasticity parameters
- Historical evidence for regime changes in interfuel substitution
- Long-run vs short-run elasticity comparison
