# Chakravorty and Tse (1999) -- Transition from Fossil Fuels to Renewable Energy

**Authors:** Ujjayant Chakravorty; Chi-Keung Woo Tse
**Institution:** Fondazione Eni Enrico Mattei (FEEM)
**Year:** 1999

## Description

Early theoretical paper on the economics of transitioning from fossil fuels to renewable
energy. SSRN returned 403 on fetch. Based on citation record, the paper models the optimal
transition path from exhaustible fossil fuels to renewable backstop energy using a Hotelling-
type resource depletion framework. Provides foundational microeconomic theory for the
fossil-to-renewable transition relevant to the project's model architecture.
